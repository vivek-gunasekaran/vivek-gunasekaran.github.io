Human Fall Detection System

Designed a system to detect any accidental fall in human beings and alert emergency care. The system can be very helpful in old age homes and hospitals where any accidental fall can be detected without any delay and prompt medical care could be provided.

The live feed from IP cameras is taken for extraction of humans from the video and it is continuously monitored for the moving objects.

A bounding box is defined over the moving body and it is checked for huge variations in the aspect ratio of the box and the fall angle. Sudden variations help us to classify it as an accidental fall.
